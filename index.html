About Us

Welcome to Salisbury Seedlings and Nursery

a family-owned business, where we combine a rich heritage spanning three generations and 50 years of experience with modern facilities to supply high-quality seedlings to customers across Australia. Our nursery is a testament to our enduring passion for agriculture, innovation, and sustainable food production.

We have dedicated greenhouses equipped with advanced climate control systems, providing the ideal conditions for nurturing healthy and robust plants. These modern facilities allow us to cultivate a diverse range of vegetable varieties, ensuring that our customers have access to a wide selection of options.

With five decades of experience in the industry, we have honed our expertise in seedling production and cultivation techniques. Our family's deep understanding of crop cycles, soil health, and plant nutrition enables us to consistently deliver top-quality seedlings that are well-suited for Australian growing conditions. We prioritize sustainability in all aspects of our operations, from seed sourcing to waste management, to minimize our environmental impact.

We invite you to join our family and experience the difference that three generations of knowledge and expertise can make.
